## Leonardo.ai Settings to Use:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1
- **Style**: Photo (for photorealistic glass effect)
- **Model**: PhotoReal v2 (or Leonardo Phoenix)
- **Guidance Scale**: 7-10
- **Quality**: High (q 2)
- **Negative Prompt**: "text, words, letters, blurry, low quality, cartoon, flat design"

## After Generation:
1. Download the highest resolution version (1024x1024 or 2048x2048)
2. Save to your Downloads folder
3. Run: `./icon-gen.sh ~/Downloads/your-image.png`
4. Open Xcode and set the AppIcon asset
